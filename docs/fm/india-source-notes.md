# India FM Source Notes

## Current Scope

The current India importer combines two official national sources:

- MIB private FM operational channels PDF:
  - `https://mib.gov.in/sites/default/files/2026-02/391_operationalchannels_01022026.pdf`
- Prasar Bharati Akashvani stations PDF:
  - `https://prasarbharati.gov.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Akashvani_Stations.pdf`

## What Is Included

- operational private FM channels published by MIB
- Akashvani / Prasar Bharati FM transmitters listed in the official station PDF
- city, state, service label, and frequency wherever exposed in those official PDFs

## Coverage Notes

This gives strong national baseline coverage for India, but it is still not the same thing as a complete all-broadcaster registry.

What is still likely missing:

- community radio where the public official source does not expose a clean national frequency table
- any services not represented in the current public MIB or Prasar Bharati PDFs

## Why This Is Still Worth Shipping

The two official PDFs together already provide broad multi-city coverage and a reproducible public import path. That makes India materially useful in the catalog without relying on unofficial directories.
